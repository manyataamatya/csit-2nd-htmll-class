Selroti Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Optional: Saffron strands for color and flavor

Preparation Steps:

  1. Combine rice flour, sugar, ghee, cardamom powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water gradually and mix until a smooth dough is formed.
  3. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into thin rotis.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and fry the rotis on both sides until golden brown.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with your favorite chutney.
