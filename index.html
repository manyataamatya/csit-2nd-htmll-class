Welcome to Selroti Recipe

Selroti is a traditional Nepali sweet rice bread, often prepared during festivals and special occasions. It is made from rice flour, sugar, and ghee, and has a unique ring shape. The batter is deep-fried to create a crispy exterior while maintaining a soft and fluffy interior. Selroti is enjoyed as a snack or dessert and is often served with tea or milk.
